Activity 3:

The activity I completed was Screen Sharing/Remote Desktop. There are several reasons why a person may need to access a computer remotely. The most common reason is for IT support to fix a problem on a laptop or desktop remotely. Another common reason is to transfer files between computers. The screen sharing program I decided to try was Chrome Remote Desktop. I looked up a Youtube video with step by step instructions on how to do it. First you need to download the Chrome Remote Desktop extension on the computer you’d like to remotely access. Then you would create a name and pin number for that computer. Next, you would go to the website https://remotedesktop.google.com/access from another computer, or download the app on your phone. From the second device, you can input the pin number and access the first device remotely. I accessed my laptop from my phone and it was actually pretty cool.
